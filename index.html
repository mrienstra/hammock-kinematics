Hammock kinematics

A frictionless pendulum bob. Velocity, acceleration, and the higher time-derivatives of position — each split into tangential and radial (centripetal) parts, plus its direction-free magnitude.

Successive time-derivatives of position: velocity (1st) → acceleration (2nd) → jerk (3rd) → snap (4th) → crackle (5th) → pop (6th).

Measure a real swing

Rest your phone on your body (stomach or between your legs is closest to your center of gravity) and let the hammock swing. Reads the gyroscope + accelerometer, then fills the simulator above.
