Only velocity's centripetal component is structurally zero — the radius never changes, so nothing points along the rope. Acceleration and jerk gain radial parts from the velocity vector turning. Watch for the instantaneous zeros too: at the ends of the swing, velocity and jerk both vanish; at the bottom, tangential acceleration and radial jerk both vanish. Period ≈ — s.
